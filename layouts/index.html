Structured business documents designed for developers

GOBL removes the pain of dealing with electronic business documents globally with a simple and easy to share JSON Schema and toolkit.

Try GOBL

Businesses waste staggering amounts of time dealing with inefficient paper documents and complex electronic formats.

Accountants have to manually extract data from paper receipts and PDFs which don't contain structured data.

Standards built with XML and text formats don't reflect the way our programming languages store or transfer data.

It's hard to understand the rules, types of taxes, and general formats required in any given market.

Dealing with this on a global scale is horrendous!

Everything you need to simplify electronic business documents, starting with invoices.

JSON Schema
Defines how to create Business Documents in JSON, the most pragmatic communication format created to date: easy to read by humans and computers alike.
Library and toolkit
Defines in code the structures and calculations required so that developers can focus on just getting the basics right.
Global tax database
Contains local taxes and validation rules, designed to reach every corner of the world.
Conversion tools
Create your invoices once using GOBL, and convert them into other local formats easily: CFDI, UBL, FacturaE, FatturaPA, etc.
Open Source & Community
We believe in open source and the power of community. We're building GOBL with the help of developers around the world.

Simple, structured, and easy to share.
